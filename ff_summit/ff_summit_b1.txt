GAME MODE: Hunted

Teams: 
Blue Civilian escorted by Red Bodyguards vs Yellow Assassins

Objectives:
The civilian must reach the van in the field at the north end of the map.

Scoring: 
Bodyguard: 10 points per escape
Assassin: 5 points per assassination

Notes: Assassins spawn with limited ammo. There is one spy, one demoman,
and one hwguy allowed on the assassin's team.

If a demoman sets a detpack near the gas tank near the VIP spawn, any
players inside the barn will be killed.
